Privacy Policy

App: MRE Site Tracker  |  Developer: NextGen Advisor  |  Last updated: March 2026

NextGen Advisor ("we", "us", or "our") built the MRE Site Tracker app. This page informs users of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and operate the app:

  • Account credentials — username and password used to log in to the app.
  • User profile data — name and assigned role (e.g. Supervisor, Planner, Manager).
  • Project and site data — construction project names, site names, locations, progress records, and schedules entered by users.
  • Work records — activity items, materials, bills of materials (BOM), key dates, design documents, and delivery schedules.
  • Photos — images captured or uploaded by users to document site progress or activities.
  • Device information — basic device data required for app functionality (operating system version, device model).

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Authenticate users and provide role-based access to project data.
  • Display and manage construction site progress, scheduling, and reporting.
  • Generate PDF reports and enable data export/backup functionality.
  • Synchronise data between the device and the project server for team collaboration.
  • Maintain app functionality and improve performance.

3. Data Storage

  • On-device storage — all project data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted local database (SQLite/WatermelonDB).
  • Server synchronisation — when sync is enabled, data is transmitted to a secure backend server (Supabase) to facilitate team collaboration. Data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Photos — captured images are stored in the device's local storage and may be uploaded to the server when sync is active.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Data is not shared except:

  • Within your organisation — project data is shared among authorised team members in the same project.
  • As required by law — if required to comply with a legal obligation or protect the rights and safety of users.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained as long as the app is installed and in use. Users or administrators may delete project data, sites, or accounts at any time from within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from the device.

6. Children's Privacy

MRE Site Tracker is intended for professional use by adults (18+) in the construction and infrastructure industry. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted local storage, HTTPS data transmission, and role-based access controls. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data held about you within the app.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We recommend reviewing this page periodically.
